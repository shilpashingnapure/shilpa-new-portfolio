The Emotional Frequency Chart shows how emotions carry distinct vibrations, influencing our energy and well-being. High-frequency emotions like love, joy, and gratitude elevate us, creating connection and vitality, while low-frequency emotions such as fear, guilt, or shame can feel heavy and draining. However, emotions are fluid, not fixed, and experiencing lower-vibration feelings is a natural part of being human. These emotions are not meant to be suppressed or feared but felt and released. Holding onto them can create stagnation, but allowing them to pass lets us maintain balance and return to a higher state of energy.

Feeling and processing emotions doesn’t lower your vibration—it’s the resistance or suppression of feelings that can weigh us down. When we acknowledge and release challenging emotions, we create space for higher-vibration states like gratitude or peace to naturally emerge. Practices such as meditation, journaling, or breathwork can help us navigate this process, keeping us aligned with our energy and potential. The chart reminds us that our vibration is dynamic, and by honoring all emotions, we can stay connected to our highest self.
